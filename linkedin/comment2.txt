The Pharma R ecosystem (https://github.com/pharmaR) historically already gathers several initiatives in this space, and others initiative such as risk.assessr (https://github.com/Sanofi-Public/risk.assessr), are active project tackling package risk assessment challenges.

Given this landscape, it might be worth considering to prioritize and support the more innovative or differentiated projects instead of overlapping with other well advance projects.
